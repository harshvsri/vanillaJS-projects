Caesar Salad

Description

Caesar salad is a classic salad made with romaine lettuce, croutons, and grated parmesan cheese, dressed with a creamy Caesar dressing. The salad is named after its creator, Caesar Cardini, who invented it in the 1920s. The dressing is made with ingredients such as egg yolks, garlic, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, and olive oil, and can be made at home or purchased pre-made. Caesar salad is a delicious and refreshing salad that can be enjoyed as a side dish or a light meal.

Ingredients

  • Romaine lettuce
  • Croutons
  • Grated parmesan cheese
  • Caesar dressing (homemade or store-bought)

Steps

  1. Wash and dry the romaine lettuce leaves and tear them into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Add the croutons and grated parmesan cheese to the lettuce.
  3. Drizzle the Caesar dressing over the salad and toss to coat evenly.
  4. Serve immediately.
