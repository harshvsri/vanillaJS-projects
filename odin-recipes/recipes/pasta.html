Pesto Pasta

Description

Pesto pasta is a simple and delicious dish made with pasta, pesto sauce, and grated parmesan cheese. Pesto sauce is made by blending fresh basil, garlic, pine nuts, parmesan cheese, and olive oil in a food processor. The sauce is then mixed with cooked pasta and topped with additional parmesan cheese. Pesto pasta is a great option for a quick and easy dinner that is full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Pesto sauce (homemade or store-bought)
  • Grated parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the pasta according to package instructions.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, prepare the pesto sauce by blending fresh basil, garlic, pine nuts, parmesan cheese, and olive oil in a food processor.
  3. Drain the cooked pasta and add it back to the pot.
  4. Add the pesto sauce to the pot and toss the pasta to coat it evenly.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste, and top with grated parmesan cheese before serving.
