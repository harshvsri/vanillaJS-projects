Chicken Stir-Fry

Description

Chicken stir-fry is a quick and easy dish made with chicken, vegetables, and a flavorful sauce. The dish is typically made by stir-frying the chicken and vegetables in a wok or large frying pan and then adding a sauce made with ingredients such as soy sauce, rice vinegar, honey, and ginger. The dish can be served over rice or noodles for a filling and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Vegetables (such as bell peppers, onions, carrots, and broccoli)
  • Soy sauce
  • Rice vinegar
  • Honey
  • Minced ginger
  • Cornstarch
  • Oil for stir-frying

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces and marinate in a mixture of soy sauce, rice vinegar, honey, minced ginger, and cornstarch for at least 15 minutes.
  2. Cut the vegetables into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Heat a wok or large frying pan over high heat and add oil for stir-frying.
  4. Add the chicken and stir-fry until cooked through,
